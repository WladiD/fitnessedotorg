  5. LastModificationTime
You may see the time that a wiki page was last modified by using the !lastmodified widget. It should be placed at the beginning of a line in the wiki text.

!lastmodified

The result of this widget is this:

Last modified anonymously on Sep. 20, 2022 at 12:16:01 p.m.
